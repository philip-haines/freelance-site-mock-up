About Me

Phil Haines is the creative mind behind York Street Designs.

Phil started his career cooking in fine dining and Michelin Stared restaurants across the globe. His talents and skills led him to places around the world such as Bangkok Thailand, Melbourne Australia, Jakarta Indonesia, and more.

After Phil left the restaurant industry he fell in love with tech and gained experience as a technical support agent. Phil continued followed his passion and attended General Assembly's User Experience Design course in October of 2019, and Flatiron School's Full Stack Web Dev course in February of 2021.
